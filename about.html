ABOUT US

THE LEGACY BEHIND THE CUP

A ROYAL BEGINNING

Banna Ri Coffee was born from a deep passion for Royal culture and an unwavering love for exceptional coffee. In the heart of Jodhpur — the Blue City — we envisioned a café that would honor the grandeur of Royal heritage while serving the finest fusion feasts and coffee concoctions.

Every corner of our café tells a story. From the hand-painted murals inspired by Mehrangarh Fort to the carefully curated menu that blends traditional Rajasthani flavors with modern café cuisine — Banna Ri Coffee is more than a café, it's a royal experience.

Our name, "Banna Ri", pays homage to the Royal bridegroom — a symbol of celebration, honor, and tradition. Every sip and bite at our café carries forward this legacy of royalty.

Our décor, music, and ambiance transport you to the era of Royal royalty. Every detail is a tribute to Rajasthan's rich cultural tapestry.
